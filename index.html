About SPLearn

SPLearn strives to provide vital information about the difficult journey ahead for humanity to underprivileged children worldwide. With technological access to many, we can help these children and everyone around them by educating them about space. We have collaborated with many seasoned astronauts who have volunteered their time and contributed into the formation of amazing courses which teach you everything, from food to managing mental health in space. By sharing SPLearn's goals, you can help thousands of children cope with the difficult times ahead.

Explore New Pathways

Learn about exciting new skills which make you comfortable in space. You'll be walked through all the pathways by veteran Astronauts who have gone to space and back. Astronauts such as Buzz Aldrin, Michael Collins, and John Young. They teach important skills such as walking on the planet, getting used to food in space and so much more.
